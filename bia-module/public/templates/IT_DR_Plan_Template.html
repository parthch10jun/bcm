6. Activation Criteria & Procedures

6.1 Activation Triggers

This DR plan shall be activated when any of the following conditions occur:

Trigger EventDescriptionActivation Level
Data Center OutageComplete loss of primary data center for >30 minutesFull Activation
Critical System FailureTier 1 system unavailable for >RTO thresholdFull Activation
Cyber AttackRansomware, data breach, or major security incidentFull Activation
Natural DisasterFire, flood, earthquake affecting IT facilitiesFull Activation
Network OutageComplete network connectivity loss for >1 hourPartial Activation
Power FailureExtended power outage beyond UPS/generator capacityPartial Activation

6.2 Activation Decision Tree

Step 1: Incident detected → Notify DR Coordinator
Step 2: Assess impact and estimated recovery time
Step 3: If recovery time > RTO → Activate DR Plan
Step 4: Notify DR Team and stakeholders
Step 5: Begin recovery procedures

6.3 Activation Checklist

  • Confirm incident severity and scope
  • Obtain activation authorization from designated authority
  • Notify all DR team members
  • Establish command center (physical or virtual)
  • Begin incident logging and documentation
  • Notify key stakeholders and management
  • Engage vendors if required
